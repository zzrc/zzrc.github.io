Climate-friendly, economical and uncomplicated cryptocurrency

Zazercoin is the next logical step on the way for ideal cryptocurrency. It's ecological, with minimal transactions fees, and simple enough for side people to understand protocol architecture.

Our mission

Provide easy access to cheap, fast, and secure crypto transactions to obtain more free and economic-savvy society. We will also consider the cases when certain results are achieved by developers inspired by our project as a successful outcome of our work. Towards the objective, we will adhere to the next values:

modernity and leadership

Continuous development is the key to prosperity and value creation. We are doing our best to comply with current standards, rules and agreements on the construction of crypto-transaction distributed systems. Over time, we are going to promote our own revolutionary ideas and methods regarding blockchain technology and related.

absolute reasonable freedom

We believe that ubiquitous centralized control, censorship and constant data collection by the governments and huge corporations are signals of the maturation of a global dictatorial empire, and you know how all empires end. Meta-technologies based on, or parallel to our system will help reduce negative impact of government and of corporations interference.

orientation towards the society

From time immemorial, the main factor around which communities were built and developed was the balance of the personal value of each individual and the common good. Now we are seeing a decline in the desire to comply with this balance at the level of government, which, in an escape for imaginary security, refuses these provisions. Our main value is this balance, and the main task is the desire to return its former strength.
